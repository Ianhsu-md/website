MY PURPOSE

  My aim as a psychiatrist is to help you find fulfilling relationships, self-esteem, authenticity, and peace in your life. My approach to treatment is built on a foundation of psychotherapy, paired with thoughtful incorporation of medications if they may be helpful for symptoms of depression, anxiety, or other psychiatric conditions.

In our work together, we will explore current difficulties but also past experiences, paying attention to things like family, culture, identity, and trauma. I believe that creating an environment of warmth, safety, and sincerity is essential to our work towards healing, change, and growth.
