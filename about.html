MY STORY

I grew up living in the American Midwest, Europe, and Asia, speaking Mandarin Chinese at home and English at school. Part of what drew me to pursue medical school was the opportunity to listen to and help those who are struggling with illness, and my later choice to specialize in psychiatry felt like a natural destination.
